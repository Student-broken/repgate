Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Data We Collect

FitLock collects minimal data to provide its core functionality:

  • Exercise statistics (push-ups, squats, screen time earned)
  • App usage data (only for apps you explicitly block)
  • Strava activity data (distance, duration) when you connect your account

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Strava activity data is fetched via API and immediately converted to credits - we do not store your Strava activities.

Third-Party Services

FitLock integrates with:

  • Google AdMob: For optional ad-based credits
  • Strava API: To sync your outdoor activities (requires your authorization)

Your Rights

You can disconnect Strava at any time from the app settings. All local data can be cleared by uninstalling the app.
